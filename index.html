About me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Linguistic and Cultural Evolution at the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology in Leipzig (Germany). My main research topics are:
phylogenetics and computational methods for exploring language history
diachronic typology
I did my BA in German linguistics at the Kyiv National Linguistic University, Ukraine, and MA in Language and Variation and German linguistics at the University of Kiel, Germany.
