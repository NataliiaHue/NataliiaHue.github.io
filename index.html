About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Linguistic and Cultural Evolution, the Max Planck Institute for Evolutionary Anthropology in Leipzig (Germany).

My main interests are:

  • phylogenetics and computational methods for exploring language history and language diversity
  • diachronic typology
  • evolution of grammar
  • language contact
  • multilingualism
  • statistics
  • data visualization

I did my BA in German linguistics at the Kyiv National Linguistic University, Ukraine, MA in Language and Variation and German linguistics at the University of Kiel and my PhD at the Friedrich Schiller University of Jena, Germany.
